Sailing for everyone!

Welcome to our sailing school, located on the beautiful Lake St. Clair in Tecumseh Ontario! We are excited to offer a variety of programs for both adults and children who are interested in learning how to sail. Our experienced instructors provide safe and engaging instruction, teaching students the skills and knowledge they need to become confident sailors. Our programs include adult sail and kid sail, as well as CANSail training for ages 9-18. Whether you're a beginner or looking to advance your skills, we have something for everyone. Come join us and experience the thrill of sailing on Lake St. Clair!

Regattas and Events!!

We are proud to anounce that South Port Sailing Club is once again hosting the 2024 Ontario Sailing Regional Championships of Western Ontario (OSRCWO), on August 17th and 18th! If interested in participating, please visit our NOR page and sign up! Classes of boats inviated include 420 s, Lasers (ILCA 7 s), and Optimists.

Additionally, we are having our annual Tuesday night Dinghy racing series this summer! Please visit our NOR Page (linked above) to sign up! Classes of boats invited include 420s, ILCA7 s (Full Rig Lasers), and Wayfarers.
