Our Team.

Welcome to our sailing team page! Our team is made up of dedicated coordinators, supervisors, instructors in training, and experienced instructors who are passionate about sharing their love for sailing with others. With years of experience both on and off the water, our team is committed to providing safe, engaging, and enjoyable sailing programs for participants of all ages and skill levels. From organizing lessons and regattas to mentoring new instructors, our team works tirelessly to ensure that every sailor has a positive and rewarding experience. Browse to learn more about our talented and enthusiastic team, and join us on the water for an unforgettable sailing adventure!

Extending our thanks...

To all of our past instructors and coordinators, we extend our heartfelt thanks for all that you have done. Your tireless efforts and commitment to excellence have helped to shape the future of our school, and we are grateful for your contributions.

As we move forward, we will continue to honor your legacy by striving for excellence in all that we do. We are proud to carry on the tradition of providing exceptional sailing education and training, and we are grateful for the foundation that has been laid by those who came before us.

Thank you again to all of our past instructors and coordinators for your invaluable contributions to South Port Sailing School. We wish you all the best in your future endeavors, and we hope that you will always remember the impact that you have had on our school and our students.
